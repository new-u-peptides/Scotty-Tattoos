How it works

Guest-spot bookings.

Slots for each city open ~8 weeks before the date. Existing waitlist is contacted first. Reach out early with reference images and rough size — I'll let you know if I can take it on.

A 30% deposit confirms your slot. Deposits are non-refundable but transferable to another date in the same city.
