The Artist

A decade of ink,

a passport full of stamps.

Scotty Massa has been tattooing professionally since 2014, building a body of work rooted in the bold lines and saturated colour of American traditional — and refined through years of guest spots across Europe, Asia and the Americas.

Every piece begins with a conversation. Your story, your placement, your style. The needle comes last.

The Process

From conversation to closing line.

A relaxed, considered approach. No surprises, no rushed designs — just careful work, made for you.

Consultation

We meet — in person at the studio or online if you're abroad. We talk through ideas, references, placement & sizing.

30–60 min · Free

Custom Design

I take your story away and come back with a one-of-one design. Two rounds of refinement included, no two pieces alike.

1–3 weeks lead

The Session

You arrive, we trace, we ink. Single or multi-session work, sterile single-use needles, vegan inks.

2–8 hours per sit

Aftercare

You leave with a wrap, a written plan, and direct contact. I check in at day 3 and day 14 — healing is part of the work.

14-day support

Style

What I tattoo.

Specialised in bold, lasting work — built to age well on skin. From American traditional to sacred geometric pieces, every tattoo is custom-built for the wearer.
