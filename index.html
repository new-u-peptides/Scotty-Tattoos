The Artist

Sixteen years in. Now all-in on geometry.

Scotty Massa has tattooed for sixteen years in studios around the world. Today he works exclusively on large-scale geometric and ornamental tattoos in Malta, from his studio in Birkirkara — drawn to the precision, the symmetry, and the way the work holds its shape and clarity over decades.

Every piece is a one-to-one collaboration — designed around you, your body and how you want to feel about it for life. No stock designs. You're part of the process from the first sketch.
