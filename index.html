The Artist

Sixteen years of ink — and a craft worth passing on.

Scotty Massa has tattooed professionally for sixteen years, building a body of work rooted in geometric and mandala design, dotwork shading and bold blackwork. He's based in Malta with select guest spots abroad — and has spent much of that time training new artists, one bench at a time.

Every piece begins with a conversation. Your story, your placement, your style. The needle comes last.
