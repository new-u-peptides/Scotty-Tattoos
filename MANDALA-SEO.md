## 1. The four intents people carry to Google for "mandala tattoo"

Search intent for mandalas splits cleanly into a funnel. Each stage wants a
different thing from the page — and a different sentence from the brand.

### A. Informational — "what does it mean / what could mine look like" (top)
The largest, earliest audience. They're deciding whether a mandala is *them*.
They want **meaning + proof of craft**, not a sales pitch.

| Query | Band | The real question underneath |
|---|---|---|
| mandala tattoo meaning / symbolism | H | "Does this stand for something I believe?" |
| mandala tattoo designs / ideas | H | "Show me range so I can find my version." |
| mandala tattoo placement — sleeve / sternum / spine / back / hand / thigh | M | "Where does it sit on a body like mine?" |
| dotwork / geometric / floral / lotus / unalome / sacred-geometry mandala | M | "Which sub-style is mine?" |
| half mandala / mandala + [flower, moon, snake] | M | "Can it be combined / personal?" |
| do mandala tattoos hurt · how long does it take | L | "What am I signing up for?" |

**Owns it:** journal/blog articles + a mandala pillar page + portfolio with
style anchors.

### B. Commercial investigation — "who should do this" (mid)
They've chosen mandala; now they're choosing an **artist**. They scan for
specialization, symmetry, line integrity, and a consistent body of work.

| Query | Band | The real question underneath |
|---|---|---|
| mandala tattoo artist · best mandala tattoo artist | M | "Who is this their *whole thing*?" |
| mandala tattoo artist near me / [city] | M–L | "Who does this near me?" |
| geometric / dotwork / sacred-geometry tattoo artist | M | "Is the technique specialist-grade?" |
| mandala sleeve specialist · custom mandala artist | L | "Can they do big, custom, symmetrical?" |

**Owns it:** Home, About (artist + philosophy), Portfolio, the mandala/geometric
service pillar.

### C. Transactional / local — "book it" (bottom)
Ready to act. They want booking clarity, price/deposit expectations, location.

| Query | Band | The real question underneath |
|---|---|---|
| book mandala tattoo · mandala tattoo consultation | L | "How do I start?" |
| custom mandala tattoo design | L | "Will it be mine, not a flash sheet?" |
| mandala tattoo price / cost | M | "What's the ballpark?" |
| mandala tattoo [Malta / city] | L | "Can I get this *here*?" |

**Owns it:** Contact / Booking, a local landing angle.

### D. Navigational / brand
`massa tattoo`, `scotty massa`, studio name. Owns it: Home, About.

## 2. The ideology, rendered from that intent

The "ideology" is the brand's belief system **stated in the words the funnel is
already searching for**. One line, five pillars — each pillar answers an intent.

> **Positioning line:**
> *Custom mandala tattoos built from sacred geometry — drawn by hand, composed
> for your body, made to outlast a trend.*

| # | Pillar (what we believe) | Says it to the searcher who wants… | Proof on the page |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Meaning over motif** — a mandala is wholeness, balance, a center; we start from symbolism. | …meaning / symbolism (A) | Short "what a mandala carries" note; symbolism in piece captions |
| 2 | **Geometry is the craft** — true symmetry, sacred-geometry construction, dotwork density. Specialist, not generalist. | …the *best* / a specialist (B) | Depth of mandala-only portfolio; technique language |
| 3 | **Composed for the body** — placement and flow designed around anatomy (sleeve, sternum, spine, back). | …placement / sleeve (A→B) | Placement-tagged portfolio; "drawn for the person" |
| 4 | **Drawn by hand, for you** — custom, consultative, sketched and refined before ink. | …custom / consultation (B→C) | Process steps; two sketch rounds; booking form |
| 5 | **Made to last** — line integrity and healing-aware design over fashion. | …quality / longevity (A,C) | Aftercare; "outlast a trend"; healed-work photos |

This is already half-written into the redesigned copy ("Marks made *with
intention*", "Composed for the body. Drawn for the person.") — pillars 3 and 4.
The move now is to make **mandala** explicit and lead pillars 1–2 harder.
