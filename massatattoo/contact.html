§ 06 — Before you book

A few things worth knowing.

How does the booking process work?
Send a request through the form with your idea, placement, and a rough size. We'll match you with the artist best suited and reach back within two working days — usually a consult, then a deposit to hold your date.
What's your deposit policy?
A non-refundable deposit secures your appointment and is applied to the final price of your tattoo. Deposits are forfeited for no-shows or reschedules with less than 48 hours' notice.
Do you take walk-ins?
Occasionally — follow us on Instagram for flash days and open chairs. For custom work, please book through the form.
How should I prepare for my appointment?
Eat well, hydrate, and skip alcohol the night before. Wear comfortable clothes that give us access to the placement, and bring headphones, snacks, and anything that helps you settle in.
Do you do cover-ups or reworks?
Sometimes — it depends on the existing piece. Send a clear, well-lit photo through the form and we'll let you know what's possible.
