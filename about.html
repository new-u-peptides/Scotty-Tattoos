Scotty Massa

Bio

Sixteen years, one needle, one obsession.

Scotty Massa has tattooed for sixteen years, working in studios across the world. After more than a decade across every style, he found his real obsession in geometry — the precision, the symmetry, the way the work draws you in without needing to explain itself.

His work lives at the intersection of bold and quiet — confident geometry that reads from across the room, built to hold its shape and clarity for decades. Today he works exclusively on large-scale custom geometric and ornamental pieces from Massa Tattoo Social Club in Birkirkara, Malta.

The work

Why geometry.

After more than a decade working across every style, I found my real obsession in geometry. There's a creative flow in this kind of tattooing I've not found anywhere else — and a longevity that matters to me. Geometric tattoos hold their shape and clarity over decades. They're still recognisable from across a room twenty years on.

Every piece I take on is a one-to-one collaboration, designed specifically for the person wearing it — built around their body, their ideas, and how they want to feel about it for the rest of their life.
