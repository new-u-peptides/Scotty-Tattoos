Scotty Massa

Bio

Sixteen years, one needle, one obsession.

Scotty Massa picked up a tattoo machine in his early twenties and never put it down. Sixteen years later, his work has been carried on bodies across four continents, built on geometric and mandala design, dotwork and bold blackwork, and shaped by mentorships from Berlin to Tokyo.

His practice lives at the intersection of bold and quiet: confident geometry that reads from across the room, paired with the patience to sit for hours on the smallest piece. These days he tattoos from Malta and trains the next wave of artists through the Massa Tattoo Social Club.

Why I travel.

A tattoo is a partnership. Some of the best partnerships I've built started with a DM from someone halfway around the world. So I started showing up: guest spots at studios in Berlin, Lisbon, Tokyo, New York. Cities I love. Friends I'd never have met otherwise.

If you can't make it to Malta, watch the tour page. Odds are I'm coming close.
